You are a treasure hunter and you want to find the Legendary Medallion. To find this treasure, you will need to go through the Atlantis and solve riddles along the way. Solving a riddle correctly will allow you to collect a piece of the Legendary Medallion and lead you to two pathways: one will be leading you to the next riddle. There will be three riddles, each increasing in difficulty. You've come across the first riddle of your journey.
